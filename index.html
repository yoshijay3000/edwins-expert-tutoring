About us

A Welcome From Edwin

I run small group classes of 1 – 4 people. If a student does not understand a concept, I will assign some questions for the other students to reinforce their understanding, while I explain the concept again. This way, no students will be bored or sitting idly by while waiting for another student.

I have been providing tutoring services for more than 12 years in fields of study ranging from Algebra 1 to Calculus, Biology to Physics, and standardized testing for the SAT and the ACT. I am confident I can help you attain your goals!
